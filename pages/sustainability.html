Our Commitment To Sustainability

We are dedicated to fostering a symbiotic relationship between travelers and the destinations they visit, ensuring a balance that benefits both.

Our sustainability

commitments

The 3P's framework

Sustainability is a core value at Chôm Chôm Travel - and to us it means much more than just using less plastic and being mindful of our carbon footprint. We are committed to the 3Ps framework - people, planet, profit. We offer eco-friendly travel but we also support local communities, ensuring every journey respects the environment and helps preserve the places you visit.

PEOPLE

We're dedicated to empowering local communities by working with passionate local guides, supporting small, locally-owned businesses, and preserving the rich cultural heritage of ethnic minorities.

PLANET

We're committed to sustainability in every step of the journey: reducing our carbon footprint, supporting eco-friendly accommodations, and ensuring that every adventure helps protect the environment.

PROFIT

We ensure that the profits from our travels directly benefit local guides and small businesses, creating a lasting, positive impact on the local economy while promoting ethical and responsible practices.
