General Information

Learn more about traveling with Chôm Chôm and the details of how we create personalized, unforgettable adventures.

What is the best time to visit Vietnam?

Vietnam offers something year-round, with the best times to visit typically being between October and April.

How is Chôm Chôm different from other travel agencies?

We specialize in creating bespoke, off-the-beaten-path journeys that connect you to the true spirit of Vietnam, with a focus on sustainability and local community support.

How do I start planning my trip?

Simply head to our ‘Build Your Own Trip’ page, fill out the inquiry form, or contact us directly for personalized assistance.

What documents do I need?
  • Valid passports minimum of 6 months before the expiration date.
  • For the most updated visa regulations for your country of origin, please refer to the official Vietnamese government website: *https://evisa.gov.vn/*.
  • There are countries that have visa exemption, you can read more about it here.
  • TIP: Make sure there are no errors in spelling all names or birthdays when completing visa application.
What is the cost of a trip?

The cost varies depending on the number of persons, activities, accommodation, transportation and the duration of the trips.

This applies to both day tours and longer trips.

For this reason, we create custom quotes for each request.

What is the minimum amount of days per trip?
You’ll find many agencies ask for a 7-day trip minimum. That’s not us. We want to make sure that everyone who comes to travel with us has a good time and great opportunities to explore Vietnam. At Chom Chom, it doesn't matter if you travel with us for half a day or 1 month, we will make sure the adventure is best tailored to your needs.
What vaccines do I need to travel into Vietnam?

No vaccinations are mandatory, but some are recommended.

Make sure you are up to date with your usual vaccinations and those required for the area you will be visiting!

  • It is recommended to be up-to-date with diphtheria-tetanus-polio (DTP) and measles-mumps-rubella (MMR) vaccinations.
  • Vaccination against tuberculosis (BCG) is also recommended, especially for longer stays.
  • Depending on local travel conditions, vaccinations against typhoid fever and viral hepatitis A and B are recommended.

For trips to isolated rural areas in Vietnam:

  • Vaccination against rabies (preventive vaccination does not dispense with curative treatment, which should be started as soon as possible in the event of proven or suspected exposure) and
  • Japanese encephalitis are strongly recommended.
Do I need insurance to travel to Vietnam?
  • Yes. The Vietnamese government requires all travellers booking with a local travel agency to have valid travel health insurance. This does not need to include trip cancellation coverage - just health-related expenses.
  • There is not specific coverage requirements, so choose the provider and policy that best suits your needs
  • If you're paying with a credit card, check with your provider, as many credit cards include some level of travel insurance.
  • As part of our standard booking confirmation process, we’ll ask you to provide proof of required health insurance. It needs to cover the full duration of your Vietnam travel dates.

Booking & Payments

Booking your trip with Chôm Chôm is simple and flexible, ensuring you have peace of mind every step of the way.

What is included in the cost of my trip?

Your investment in a Chôm Chôm journey covers much more than just logistics. It includes:

  • Personalized travel consultation.
  • Custom-designed, private itineraries.
  • Local expertise and insider insights.
  • Full booking management (transport, accommodation, experiences).
  • On-call support from your Adventure Coordinator.
  • Access to exclusive guides and partners.
  • Sustainable travel practices that support local communities.
What payment methods do you accept?
  • We accept payment by bank transfer to a Vietnamese bank account as a local company.
  • OnePay (international credit card payments).
  • Please note: processing fees may apply depending on your card provider.
Why do I need to provide ID when booking?
To confirm your itinerary and issue service contracts, we require a copy of your passport ID page. This helps us secure your reservations accurately and in compliance with Vietnamese travel regulations.
What happens if I need to cancel my trip last minute?
We understand plans can change. Please refer to our full Terms & Conditions document for our updated cancellation policy, including timelines and refund procedures that were sent as part of your contract. Your Adventure Coordinator is also available to help you manage any unforeseen changes.
